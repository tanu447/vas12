Disclaimer

A university disclaimer typically serves as a legal statement that limits the university's liability and clarifies certain aspects of its services, responsibilities, and information provided. It informs students, staff, and other stakeholders about the limitations, conditions, and risks involved in engaging with the university’s programs, websites, and services. The disclaimer will note that the university’s website and other digital platforms (such as learning management systems) are provided for informational purposes, and the university does not guarantee that the website will always be available or error-free. Technical issues, maintenance, or system upgrades can affect access to the website or other online resources, and the university is not responsible for such interruptions. The university may provide access to third-party resources, including external websites, research, or events. The disclaimer will clarify that the university is not responsible for the content, services, or actions of third-party websites or organizations, and students and staff engage with such third-party materials at their own risk. The disclaimer may reference the university’s Student Code of Conduct or similar policies, emphasizing that students are expected to act responsibly and adhere to academic and behavioral standards. The university is not responsible for inappropriate behavior by students, faculty, or staff and reserves the right to take disciplinary action in cases of misconduct, including suspension or expulsion.


  • 1. States the purpose of the disclaimer, which is to limit the university's liability and clarify terms for students, staff, and other users of university services or resources.
  • 2. Clarifies that academic programs, courses, and related content may change without notice. The university cannot guarantee that all details will remain accurate or unchanged.
  • 3. Limits the university's responsibility for personal injury, property damage, or any loss or harm arising from participation in university programs or use of university facilities and services.
  • 4. States that the university is not liable for external links, third-party websites, or services linked from the university's platforms. The university does not control the content of third-party resources.
  • 5. Specifies that the university makes no guarantee about the availability or uninterrupted access to its website or online resources. Technical issues may occur, and the university is not liable for downtime.
  • 6. Clarifies that students are expected to follow the university’s code of conduct and that the university is not responsible for any inappropriate behavior or misconduct by students, staff, or visitors.
  • 7. Indicates that the university owns intellectual property related to its educational content and resources. Students and staff may have limited rights to use or share this content, and unauthorized use is prohibited.
  • 8. Refers to the university's privacy policy and states that the university is not liable for data breaches or for third-party services with differing privacy policies.
  • 9. Includes a clause to protect the university from liability in case of unforeseen events, such as natural disasters, that prevent the university from fulfilling its obligations.
  • 10. Explains that the university may update or modify the disclaimer at any time and that users should regularly check for any updates. The university may notify users of significant changes.
